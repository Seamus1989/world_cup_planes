# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Planes ([planes.studio](https://www.planes.studio/)) staff — a creative studio. Internal and social,
played over the six weeks of the 2026 World Cup. Two contexts: **daily on a phone** (quick "how are my
teams doing" checks, Slack banter) and **one big-screen moment** — the live team-assignment reveal at a
team meeting. Mixed football knowledge; the fun is office rivalry, not expertise.

## Product Purpose

A £5-a-seat office sweepstake game for the 2026 World Cup. Players are drawn a country, watch the
tournament unfold around their teams, chase side-quest prizes (Golden Boot, best predictor, Wooden
Spoon), and trash-talk via a Slack bot. Success = everyone stays engaged for six weeks — *even after
their team is knocked out* — and the reveal is a genuine "wow" moment on the big screen.

## Brand Personality

Playful, competitive, a touch cheeky — but crafted, not gimmicky. The whole product lives in an airport
**"Departures"** world: boarding passes, split-flap boards, gates, the Tannoy announcer. Three words:
**spirited, mechanical, premium-arcade.** It should feel like something someone lovingly built for the
office, not a corporate intranet.

## Anti-references

- Corporate intranet / a sweepstake run off a shared spreadsheet.
- Generic SaaS dashboard: card grids, the big-number hero-metric template, gradient accents.
- Betting-app aesthetics: neon casino green, aggressive odds, flashing CTAs.
- The generic "3D globe with glowing lines" crypto/AI landing-page cliché. Ours is a *departures hall*,
  not a network-graph hero.

## Design Principles

- **Departures, everywhere.** Every state maps to the airport metaphor — flights, gates, boarding,
  delayed, cancelled. The metaphor is the system, not decoration.
- **The board is the hero.** Show data as a living split-flap board, not tables.
- **Earn the wow.** The reveal and key moments get a real motion budget; everything else stays calm,
  fast and legible.
- **Phone-glanceable, big-screen-dramatic.** The same data reads at arm's length on mobile and from
  across a room on a projector.
- **Stay in it after you're out.** Knocked-out players still have side-quests, predictions and banter.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast on all text (ink and amber on tarmac verified ≥ 4.5:1).
- Full `prefers-reduced-motion` fallbacks — the reveal degrades to crossfades/instant; no essential
  information is conveyed by motion alone.
- Never status-by-color-alone: pair every status with an icon and a word (✓ BOARDING, ✗ CANCELLED).
